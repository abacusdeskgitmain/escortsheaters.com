QUALITY CONTROL

For more than 40 years, customers have relied on ESCORTS for the utmost in quality and innovative solutions for industrial heating applications.   We source only the best raw material from renowned suppliers all across the world.  Resistance alloys, tubes, MgO powder (insulation material) etc. are sourced only from our overseas suppliers.    Our stringent quality control has enabled us to retain our position as the brand leader in the country.  Our standard range of products carries the ISI mark and the Escorts Heating Element Division has ISO 9001:2008 certification from URS

QUALITY POLICY

We commit to supply Heating Elements and Heating units to our end users by understanding their intended use, meeting their specifications and exceeding their expectations, in terms of delivery and performance.  We commit to adhere and improve the processes that constitute our Quality Management System.
