About Us

Escorts Limited, a group of companies manufacturing tractors, Automotive Components, construction equipments, Railway Equipments etc. are the pioneers and acknowledged leaders in the field of Heaters and Heating Systems, in India. With more than 40 years of manufacturing and marketing experience and clientele running to thousands in every field, individual customer’s needs are guaranteed to be met to their complete satisfaction. Initially, Escorts started manufacturing of heaters in collaboration with M/s. ELTRA of then West Germany.

Escorts manufacture a wide range of standard heating elements which are marketed through an All India dealer net-work for fastest off-the-shelf delivery.

Escorts also manufacture various types of custom built heaters and heating systems to meet specific requirement of individual customers for heating Air, Water, Oil, Chemicals etc.

We have the following products to offer:

  • Immersion heating elements for Water, Oil, and Chemicals heating.
  • Air heating elements
  • Fuel gas and process gas heaters.
  • Regeneration / Reactivation heaters
  • Process Heaters
  • Circulation Heaters
  • Large heating systems with explosion proof terminal box and thyristor control panels.
  • Heaters for ESP and Ash handling systems
  • Boiler pre-heaters
  • Heating elements for appliances.
  • Space Heaters

Our heating elements carry the prestigious : 

  • IS:4159 mark
  • CMRS Dhanbad certificate  for flameproof heating units.
  • ISO 9001:2008

We, at Escorts, believe in product quality and to ensure better quality, we source best raw materials available.   Major raw materials like MgO Powder (Insulation material) and tubes are sourced from our overseas suppliers. State of the art  machineries,  stringent quality control and continuous improvements in construction and raw materials help us to maintain the position of Brand Leader in Heating Element market.

Shell/Vessels for heating units are designed & manufactured as per pressure vessel codes like ASME Sec VIII Div I with or without U stamp in close coordination with our associates who are expert in this field.
